As a Philosophy grad student at Bologna University, I found myself attending more frequently cinema and communication courses rather than my own.

-


-

During the Erasmus experience in Paris, I was introduced to intersectional feminism and to the Feminist Film Theory. This is why I am now orienting my studies towards subjects such as: male gaze and female gaze, cis gaze and trans gaze, the passionate detachment theory and francophone directors such as Céline Sciamma, Agnès Varda and Chantal Akerman.


Since 2020 I have been working between Forlì and Bologna as a photographer and videomaker. I take care of shooting and editing short-movies, music videos and promotional videos, often in collaboration with other production companies.
